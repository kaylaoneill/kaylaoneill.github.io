My Past Research

While studying at Trent University, I had the privlegde to study a research project being conducted by The Friends of Muskoka Watershed. This reseach project looked at the effects of calcium deficiencies in the Muskoka forest. These deficiencies had been brought on by human acttions such as farming but also through acid rain. The Friends of Muskoka Watershed was convienced that using the woodash that Muksoka residents brunt in their home fireplaces and kitchens, we could revert some of the calcium deficiencies in the forests.
